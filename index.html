Dunia Salama Foundation

About Us

Dunia Salama Foundation (DUSAFO) is a Non-government Organization with registration #00NGO/R/2064.Dunia Salama Foundation strive to see the society live in peace and harmony through the right use of digital and internet to promote quality education for Children and Youth. In Tanzania, majority of people use internet but without full understanding of its opportunities and threats hidden in it especially in creating a safe world for all or disrupting it. DUSAFO is ambitious for the best and positive changes in the society where children, women and youth live a peaceful life. If peace prevails among these special groups; engaging them in promotion of education, environment environmental preservation, civic engagement and health will have a meaning.

Our Mission

DUSAFO mission is to educate the community on proper use of digital and internet, Supporting Children on digital with school materials, Community knowledge Centers for all And Peace promotion.

Our Vision

A world where peace and tranquility are prevailing.

Organization Objectives
  1. To create enabling environment for the community to use digital and internet for the benefit of the current and future generation
  2. To give support for the children in school
  3. To improve the environment for the most Vulnerable Children to equally access education for liberation of their families
  4. To promote the community members to access to knowledge through community-based Knowledge centers
  5. To promote useful cultural practices through the use of arts like poetry, carving, and other traditional ways in line with internet
